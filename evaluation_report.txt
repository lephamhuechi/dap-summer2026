── Key insights ──
  1. Beauty & Home (inelastic): có thể tăng giá +18–24% mà không mất nhiều CR
  2. Electronics & Books (elastic): rất nhạy cảm với giá, chỉ tăng +2–7%
  3. Dynamic pricing tổng thể mang lại revenue lift tích cực so với fixed
  4. Margin cải thiện ở tất cả categories khi áp dụng optimal pricing

── Dynamic pricing rules (final) ──

  [Beauty, Home & Kitchen]  inelastic  β ∈ [-0.87 to -1.19]
    → Tăng +18–24% · Max cap +30% · Trigger khi stock thấp hoặc competitor tăng giá

  [Clothing, Sports, Toys]  moderate  β ∈ [-1.19 to -1.55]
    → Tăng +10–14% · A/B test weekly · Volume discount khi cart > ₹1,000

  [Electronics]  elastic  β ∈ [-1.82]
    → Tăng tối đa +7% · Price match competitor trong 24h · Flash sale -10% khi tồn kho cao

  [Books]  very elastic  β ∈ [-2.10]
    → Giữ ±2% market rate · Bundle pricing (mua 2 giảm thêm 5%) thay vì tăng đơn giá

── Model evaluation summary ──
  Model: RandomForestRegressor (n=200, max_depth=8)
  Target: adjusted_price (demand×supply multiplier applied)
  Features: log_price, discount_pct, category_encoded,
            month, day_of_week, purchase_count, margin_pct
  Expected R²: 0.85–0.95 (target is derived from features,
               so high R² is expected — validate on held-out period)
  Recommendation: re-train quarterly with fresh transaction data
